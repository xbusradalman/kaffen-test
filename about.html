About Kaffen

New London Coffee Founded For Extraordinary Test

Kaffen is a cozy meeting point where great coffee comes together with a simple and friendly atmosphere. We serve freshly brewed coffee at any time of the day, along with carefully prepared sweet and savory bites in a relaxed setting. Our goal is to create a warm place where everyone who walks in feels comfortably at home.

  • Natural Coffee Beans
    Naturally processed, freshly roasted coffee beans with their full aroma carefully preserved.
  • 100% ISO Certification
    Production processes audited to meet strict international food safety and quality standards.
